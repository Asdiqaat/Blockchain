Empowering You to Secure Your Digital Documents Forever

VaultStamp is built on the belief that every individual and organization deserves digital confidence. We aim to make cryptographic proof and data verification accessible to all — secure, simple, and decentralized.

By combining the power of the Internet Computer Protocol (ICP) and client-side SHA-256 hashing, VaultStamp never stores your files — only their hash. This ensures complete privacy while enabling permanent proof of existence via smart contracts written in Motoko.

We replace fragile and centralized timestamping solutions with a public, tamper-proof mechanism. Users can verify a document’s authenticity by simply matching its hash with what’s recorded on-chain — ensuring your records remain untouchable and provable, forever.
